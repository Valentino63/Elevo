Privacy Policy — Elevo

Last updated: 13 July 2026

Elevo is a self-improvement and fitness tracking app for iPhone. This policy explains, in plain terms, what happens to your information when you use it. The short version: everything you enter stays on your own device, and none of your personal information ever leaves it.

All of your data — your task completions, XP and streaks, records, workouts, and archetype choices — is stored locally on your device using your iPhone's own storage. Elevo has no servers, no cloud backup, and no user accounts. There is nothing to sign up for and nowhere for your personal information to be sent, because none of it is ever transmitted off your device.

Elevo does not collect, share, or sell any of your data. There are no analytics or tracking tools, no advertising, and no third-party services that receive your personal information. No one — not us, not any outside company — can see what you record in Elevo. Your information stays private to you. (The app is built with the Expo framework, which may make routine technical connections such as checking for app updates; these never include any of your personal information.)

Because your data lives only on your device, you are always in full control of it. If you delete Elevo from your iPhone, all data the app stored is permanently deleted along with it. There is no separate copy anywhere for us to hold, recover, or hand over.

As an EU-based developer, I take the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) seriously. In practice, GDPR gives you rights over your personal data — to access it, correct it, delete it, and control how it is used. With Elevo those rights are met by design: since the app never collects or transmits your personal data and keeps everything on your own device, you already hold and manage all of it directly, and there is no personal data held by me or anyone else to request or erase.

If this policy ever changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new "last updated" date. If you have any questions about your privacy or this policy, you can reach me at elevoappteam@gmail.com.
